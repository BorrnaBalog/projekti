Superior Accommodation
surrounded by nature - Villa Gabriel

The property has a private entrance and safe and secure parking for all of our guests. The grounds at Villa Gabriel feature a private heated swimming pool, children’s playground, a garden and a shaded pergola. The terrace area surrounding the pool and the upper lounge terrace are equipped with luxurious and comfortable sunbathing loungers. You will also find a lovely dining set for ten and a barbeque. Wi-Fi and parking are complimentary. We can arrange pick up/drop off to/from the airport. The bright and elegant living room and sitting area are furnished with contemporary furniture, lighting and carpets. Both have access to the terrace and the swimming pool. The open plan formal dining space connects with a modern, very well equipped kitchen. There is one en-suite bedroom on this floor.

The area below the ground floor, basement, consists of a wine cellar area, gym, pool table, sauna and toilette.

At Villa Gabriel sweet dreams are made of maximum comfort and untouched nature. Each of our en-suite bedrooms has been designed and equipped in a manner of a five star hotel. When staying with us, good dreams do come true. Villa Gabriel has four spacious en-suite bedrooms, one on the ground floor and three on the first floor.

We can accommodate up to 10 guests. We are pet friendly. The house is completely air-conditioned. In total the residence has five bathrooms spread over three living levels. There is private parking on the grounds. Free Wi-Fi and satellite TVs are provided.

Four modern designed en-suite bedrooms, spacious living and dining areas plus playroom with the pool table, sauna and fully-equipped kitchen;

Villa Gabriel is an ideal destination for a family or group of friends. Pet friendly house.
